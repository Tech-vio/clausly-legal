1. Overview

Clausly ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Clausly application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Document Content

  • Type: Legal documents and contracts you upload
  • Purpose: To provide AI-powered analysis services
  • Processing: Sent via encrypted connection to AI services for analysis
  • Storage: Analysis results are cached locally on your device

2.2 Usage Statistics

  • Type: App usage patterns, feature usage frequency, performance data
  • Purpose: To improve app performance and user experience
  • Storage: Stored only locally on your device
  • Anonymization: Does not include personally identifiable information

2.3 Subscription Information

  • Type: Purchase records, membership status, subscription type
  • Purpose: To manage your membership benefits and billing
  • Processing: Processed through Apple App Store
  • Storage: Cached locally on your device

2.4 Device Permissions

  • Camera Permission: Used to scan paper documents
  • Photo Library Permission: Used to select document images from your device
  • Control: You can revoke these permissions at any time in system settings

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide AI document analysis services
  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Process subscriptions and billing
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except for:

4.1 Service Providers

To provide service functionality, we share necessary data with the following types of service providers:

  • AI Analysis Services: For intelligent document analysis (data transmitted encrypted, original documents not stored)
  • Cloud Services: For user authentication and data storage (data stored encrypted)
  • Payment Processing: Subscription payments processed through Apple App Store

All third-party service providers commit to protecting your data security and only use your data within the scope necessary to provide services.

4.2 Legal Requirements

When required by law or legally requested by government authorities

5. Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted Transmission: All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Local Storage: Sensitive data is primarily processed locally on your device
  • Minimization Principle: We only collect necessary data
  • Regular Cleanup: Automatic cleanup of expired cached data

6. Data Retention

  • Document Content: Not retained on our servers after analysis completion
  • Analysis Results: Retained on your device; you can delete them at any time
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymized data retained for 12 months
  • Subscription Records: Retained for 7 years as required by tax law

7. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: View information we hold about you
  • Correction: Correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Data Portability: Obtain your data in a machine-readable format

8. Children's Privacy

Clausly is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in locations outside your country/region. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

10. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated to you through in-app notifications or other appropriate means.
